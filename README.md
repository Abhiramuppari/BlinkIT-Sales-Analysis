ğŸ“ˆ Key Insights

Regular-fat products contribute significantly more to sales than low-fat items.

Essential categories such as snacks, fruits, vegetables, and household items drive most revenue.

Medium-sized outlets are the highest revenue generators.

Tier 3 locations outperform Tier 1 and Tier 2 in total sales.

Supermarket Type 1 outlets lead across almost all KPIs.

Newer outlets (post-2015) show improved sales performance and stability.

Customer ratings average 3.9, indicating potential areas for service improvement.

Enhance customer experience to improve the overall rating from 3.9+.
